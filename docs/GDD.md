# GDD: The Waning Light

## Core Concept
Solo fantasy hexcrawl about survival, exploration, and outrunning the Searing — an unstoppable wall of heat consuming the world. You are a Cinder-Seeker traveling the Twilight Strip, managing Supply, Hope, and Health across a procedurally generated hex map where every hex is tagged with biome, material, feature, and history traits that drive encounters.
